Welcome to AVS English Pubic School

About The School

Arunodaya Vidya Samsthe is the humble, dedicated and highly motivated community to enable students to become the global stars of the future. Our school's mission is to prepare kids for the life but not to make them rote learners. Our mission is to provide a safe, disciplined learning environment that empowers all students to develop their full potential. We are serving mainly on the K-10 Space catering various subjects of Karnataka state board. We are also providing a hybrid curriculum for grades Montessori to4th. The school is situated close to the heart of IT hub-electronic city making it one of the best schools in the electronic city AVS has a great community encompassing keen learners, , parents, core teaching staff and a great support staff.



All our teachers are highly qualified and are able to work in a challenging environment. The school started marching with its baby steps in the year 2005, since then our tem, is making every effort to reach Excellence in both academics and the holistic development of each child. As the saying goes- today`s children are tomorrow`s citizens, we are much goal-oriented, and are working to develop a highest level of integrity, passion and commitment in the students while they are still young. Our curriculum is so made that, the students can gain a culture of excellence that includes variety of skills in the literacy through start classes, team-based learning, discussion, seminar presentations, exhibitions, debates, essay writing and most importantly the motor activities related to their syllabus. Our team has a motto to integrate the English Language, along with our mother tongue. Additionally, science, technology, value based subjects that helps to gain strong culture and climate is given the highest importance.
